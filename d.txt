And the Lord spake unto Moses, saying,
Speak unto all the congregation of the children of Israel,
And say unto them, Ye shall be holy:
For I the Lord your God am holy.
Ye shall fear every man his mother, and his father,
And keep my sabbaths: I am the Lord your God.
Turn ye not unto idols, nor make to yourselves molten gods:
I am the Lord your God.
And if ye offer a sacrifice of peace offerings unto the Lord,
Ye shall offer it at your own will.
It shall be eaten the same day ye offer it, and on the morrow:
And if ought remain until the third day, it shall be burnt in the fire.
